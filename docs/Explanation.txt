1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

The Loan Approval System is a production-grade Multi-Agent Agentic AI platform
that automates loan application evaluation using Claude Sonnet 4.6. The system
analyzes applicant profiles, financial risk, and synthesizes decisions into
APPROVED, REJECTED, or MANUAL_REVIEW classifications with clear explanations.

Business Objectives:
- Automate loan approval decisions to improve speed and consistency
- Reduce manual review overhead through intelligent pre-screening
- Provide explainable, auditable decisions for compliance
- Enable live modification of decision rules during evaluation

Key Capabilities:
- Processes complete loan applications in ~2 seconds
- Evaluates 10+ risk factors across 4 specialized agents
- Generates audit trails for regulatory compliance
- Provides transparent reasoning for all decisions

3. AGENT SPECIFICATIONS & RESPONSIBILITIES
================================================================================

3.1 APPLICANT PROFILE AGENT
────────────────────────────
Role: Analyze applicant demographics, income stability, and employment

Input:
  - Applicant ID, Age, Income, Employment Type
  - Employment Years, Location
  - Optional: Credit history from database

Output Schema:
  {
    "analysis": "Narrative analysis of profile",
    "findings": {
      "income_stability_score": 0.85,
      "employment_risk": "LOW",
      "credit_history_summary": "10 years, excellent payment history"
    },
    "risk_level": "LOW|MEDIUM|HIGH|CRITICAL",
    "confidence": 0.85,
    "reasoning": "Clear explanation of findings"
  }

Decision Logic:
- Income < $25k → HIGH risk
- Employment < 1 year → MEDIUM risk
- Credit score < 650 → HIGH risk
- 5+ years employment → LOW risk boost
- Self-employed → +0.15 risk multiplier

Tools:
- fetch_credit_history: Access applicant credit profile
- verify_employment: Confirm employment details


3.2 FINANCIAL RISK AGENT
────────────────────────
Role: Calculate financial risk metrics and detect anomalies

Input:
  - Income, Credit Score, Loan Amount
  - Existing Liabilities, Tenure
  - Monthly payment calculations

Output Schema:
  {
    "analysis": "Financial risk assessment",
    "findings": {
      "dti_ratio": 30.5,
      "credit_risk_level": "LOW",
      "loan_to_income_ratio": 3.1
    },
    "risk_level": "LOW|MEDIUM|HIGH|CRITICAL",
    "confidence": 0.80,
    "reasoning": "Explanation of risk factors"
  }

Decision Logic:
DTI Ratios:
  - < 20% → Excellent (LOW risk)
  - 20-30% → Good (LOW risk)
  - 30-43% → Fair (MEDIUM risk)
  - > 43% → Poor (HIGH risk)

Credit Score Tiers:
  - >= 750 → Excellent (LOW risk)
  - 700-749 → Good (LOW risk)
  - 650-699 → Fair (MEDIUM risk)
  - 600-649 → Poor (HIGH risk)
  - < 600 → Critical (CRITICAL risk)

Anomaly Flags:
  - Loan/Income > 5x → High anomaly
  - Loan/Income 3-5x → Elevated anomaly
  - Income < $25k → Low income flag

Tools:
- calculate_dti_ratio: DTI = (monthly_obligations / monthly_income) * 100
- assess_credit_risk: Map credit score to risk level
- detect_anomalies: Flag unusual patterns


3.3 DECISION AGENT
──────────────────
Role: Synthesize all factors and produce final classification

Input:
  - Profile analysis results
  - Financial risk analysis results
  - Application data
  - Business constraints

Output Schema:
  {
    "analysis": "Multi-criteria synthesis",
    "classification": "APPROVED|REJECTED|MANUAL_REVIEW",
    "confidence": 0.85,
    "risk_score": 25,
    "factors": ["Good credit", "Low DTI", "Stable employment"],
    "reasoning": "Decision justification"
  }

Decision Criteria:
  APPROVED:
    - Risk score < 40
    - Confidence > 0.7
    - No critical business rule violations

  REJECTED:
    - Risk score > 70
    - Confidence > 0.7 for rejection
    - Critical failures: credit < 600, DTI > 50, age concerns

  MANUAL_REVIEW:
    - Risk score 40-70
    - Confidence between 0.4-0.7
    - Borderline cases requiring human judgment

Risk Score Calculation:
  base_risk = (profile_risk_weight * 0.3) + (financial_risk_weight * 0.7)
  adjustments:
    - DTI > 43%: +15 points
    - Credit < 650: +15 points
    - Anomalies: +5 points per anomaly (max +15)
  final_risk = min(100, base_risk)

Tools:
- synthesize_factors: Aggregate all risk factors
- generate_explanation: Create decision justification
- calculate_confidence: Score decision confidence


3.4 COMPLIANCE & ACTION ORCHESTRATOR AGENT
──────────────────────────────────────────
Role: Document decision, create audit trails, generate notifications

Input:
  - Complete decision information
  - Application data
  - Decision justification
  - Regulatory requirements

Output Schema:
  {
    "case_id": "CASE-20240619-ABC123",
    "audit_entry": "Logged to audit trail",
    "notification_sent": true,
    "compliance_notes": "Regulatory compliance verified",
    "summary": "Summary of actions taken"
  }

Actions:
- Create unique case ID: CASE-{YYYYMMDD}-{UUID}
- Log decision to immutable audit trail
- Generate applicant notification (email template)
- Create compliance case file
- Track decision timestamp and reasoning

Audit Trail Content:
  - Applicant ID
  - Decision (APPROVED/REJECTED/MANUAL_REVIEW)
  - Risk score and confidence
  - Decision factors
  - Full reasoning
  - Timestamp
  - System version
  - Decision agent responses


4. ORCHESTRATION FLOW (LangChain Sequential)
================================================================================

The LoanOrchestrator manages the complete workflow:

Step 1: VALIDATE INPUT
  - Check all required fields present
  - Validate data types and ranges
  - Apply basic business rules
  → If invalid: Return error immediately

Step 2: PARALLEL AGENT EXECUTION
  Simultaneously run:
  - Applicant Profile Agent
  - Financial Risk Agent
  (Enables 2x speedup vs sequential)
  → Aggregate results

Step 3: APPLY MCP BUSINESS RULES
  - Check age (18-100)
  - Verify credit score minimum (600)
  - DTI ratio thresholds
  - Loan/income ratios
  → Flag violations

Step 4: DECISION SYNTHESIS
  - Pass profile + risk results to Decision Agent
  - Synthesize into classification + factors
  - Generate explanation + confidence score
  → Return structured decision

Step 5: COMPLIANCE PROCESSING
  - Create audit entry
  - Generate case file
  - Schedule notifications
  - Log to immutable audit trail
  → Return final response

Step 6: RETURN COMPLETE DECISION
  {
    "application_id": "APP...",
    "classification": "APPROVED|REJECTED|MANUAL_REVIEW",
    "confidence": 0.85,
    "risk_score": 25,
    "factors": [...],
    "reasoning": "...",
    "case_id": "CASE-...",
    "audit_entry": {...}
  }

Total Processing Time: ~2 seconds (with LLM calls)


5. MCP SERVER ARCHITECTURE
================================================================================

MCP servers provide standardized tool interfaces for agents. Each server:
- Exposes 2-4 domain-specific tools
- Returns consistent JSON schemas
- Handles errors gracefully
- Logs all operations

5.1 ApplicantDB Server
──────────────────────
Tools:
  get_credit_history(applicant_id) → credit history object
  verify_employment(employment_type, years) → employment status
  validate_applicant_data(data) → completeness check
  get_applicant_profile(id) → full applicant record

Response Schema:
  {
    "status": "success|error",
    "data": { tool-specific data },
    "error": "Error message if status=error"
  }

5.2 RiskRulesDB Server
──────────────────────
Tools:
  calculate_dti_ratio(monthly_income, monthly_obligations) → DTI + risk level
  assess_credit_risk(credit_score) → risk category
  detect_anomalies(loan_amount, annual_income) → anomaly flags
  apply_business_rules(application_data) → rule violations

Example Call:
  calculate_dti_ratio(5000, 1000)
  → { "dti_ratio": 20.0, "risk_level": "LOW", ... }

5.3 DecisionSynthesis Server
────────────────────────────
Tools:
  synthesize_factors(profile_risk, financial_risk, ...) → recommendation
  generate_explanation(decision, factors, risk_score, confidence) → text
  calculate_confidence_score(profile_strength, financial_strength, anomalies)

5.4 NotificationSystem Server
─────────────────────────────
Tools:
  create_audit_entry(applicant_id, decision, reasoning) → entry_id
  generate_notification(applicant_id, decision, reason) → notification text
  log_decision(applicant_id, case_id, decision, risk_score, confidence)
  create_case_file(applicant_id, decision, application_data, analysis)

7. EXPLAINABILITY & DECISION TRANSPARENCY
================================================================================

Every decision includes:

1. CLASSIFICATION
   - APPROVED: Loan approved with conditions
   - REJECTED: Application rejected, can reapply after 90 days
   - MANUAL_REVIEW: Escalated to human reviewer

2. CONFIDENCE SCORE (0-1)
   - 0.9+: Very confident decision
   - 0.7-0.9: High confidence
   - 0.5-0.7: Moderate confidence (manual review candidate)
   - <0.5: Low confidence (requires review)

3. RISK SCORE (0-100)
   - 0-40: Low risk (favorable for approval)
   - 40-70: Medium risk (borderline)
   - 70+: High risk (favorable for rejection)

4. DECISION FACTORS (List of strings)
   Examples:
   - "Good credit score (750)"
   - "Low DTI ratio (20%)"
   - "Stable 5+ year employment"
   - "Income supports loan amount"
   - "No recent anomalies"

5. REASONING (Narrative explanation)
   "Applicant meets approval criteria with strong financial profile.
    Credit score of 750 indicates excellent payment history. DTI ratio
    of 20% is well within acceptable limits. Employment stability shows
    5+ years with same employer. Overall risk assessment is LOW."

6. AUDIT TRAIL
   - Case ID for tracking
   - Timestamp of decision
   - All agent responses
   - Rule violations (if any)
   - Full decision history

Example Full Decision:
  {
    "application_id": "APP123",
    "classification": "APPROVED",
    "confidence": 0.85,
    "risk_score": 25,
    "factors": [
      "Excellent credit score (750)",
      "Low DTI ratio (20%)",
      "Stable 5+ year employment",
      "Reasonable loan-to-income (3.1x)",
      "No anomalies detected"
    ],
    "reasoning": "Applicant demonstrates strong financial profile meeting
                  all approval criteria. Credit history is excellent with
                  no late payments. Income stability is proven through 5+
                  years employment. Debt service is manageable at 20% DTI.
                  Loan amount is appropriate relative to income.",
    "case_id": "CASE-20240619-ABC123",
    "compliance_notes": "Compliant with all regulations",
    "audit_entry": {...}
  }
